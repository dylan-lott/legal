# Privacy Policy — Lott PT Claude Integration

**Last updated:** April 5, 2026

## Overview

Lott PT Claude Integration is a private, internal application used solely by Lott Physical Therapy ("Lott PT") to access its own QuickBooks Online financial data for business operations, reporting, and data pipeline management.

## Data Collection and Use

This application accesses QuickBooks Online accounting and payment data belonging to Lott PT. No third-party user data is collected, stored, or processed. All data accessed through this integration is used exclusively for internal business purposes.

## Data Sharing

Lott PT does not sell, share, or distribute any financial data accessed through this integration to any third parties.

## Data Storage

Data accessed through this integration may be stored in Lott PT's private Snowflake data warehouse and internal systems, secured with industry-standard encryption and access controls.
